# Screenshot Capture Guide

This guide provides strategic instructions for capturing high-impact product screenshots from the running MessageMind AI application. Use these for pitch deck slides and as thumbnail visuals for your video walkthrough.

---

## 1. Dashboard: The "Command Center" State
**Goal**: Show a thriving platform with high-impact numbers and real-time signal processing.

- **KPI Targets**:
    - Revenue Recovery: **$42.8k**
    - AI Conv. Confidence: **88%**
    - Fatigue Alerts: **12**
- **Action**: Hover over the "Revenue Recovery" chart to show the tooltip with specific daily spikes.
- **Crop**: Capture the full top-strip of KPI cards and the "Revenue Uplift" comparison chart.

---

## 2. AI Decision Engine: The "ML Intelligence" State
**Goal**: Prove the platform detects individual user behavior and switches tactics instantly.

- **Seeded Persona**: Select the **"Bargain Hunter"** persona.
- **Expected Visuals**: 
    - Conversion probability should spike to **~89%**.
    - The "Optimized Channel" should switch to **"SMS with Discount Code"**.
    - The prompt history should show a "User Fatigue" check passing.
- **Capture**: The central "Intelligence Core" panel and the "Channel Recommendation" chip (Neon Blue).

---

## 3. Chat Simulator: The "Real-Time Interaction" State
**Goal**: Show the end-result of a decision in a human-readable format.

- **Scenario**: Simulate an **"Abandoned High-Value Cart"** event.
- **Visuals**: The simulator window should display a message bubble with a personalized discount and a clear CTA link.
- **Capture**: The simulator mobile-frame and the "Live Latency" tag in the bottom right (**< 10ms**).

---

## 4. Dataset Explorer: The "Real Data" Proof
**Goal**: Dispel the idea that this is "just a frontend" by showing raw data ingestion.

- **State**: Scroll to the center of the data table.
- **Visuals**: Ensure columns like `purchase_completed`, `product_category`, and `time_of_day` are visible.
- **Capture**: The table view alongside the "Pipeline Status: Active" badge.

---

## 5. Privacy Governance: The "Trust" State
**Goal**: Showcase enterprise-grade compliance.

- **Action**: Toggle the "Real-time PII Stripping" switch (if available) or focus on the audit log.
- **Visuals**: Show an entry like `User ID: 88...21 | PII Scrubbed | Decision Served`.
- **Capture**: The "Consent Pipeline" visualization.

---

## Screenshot Composition Tips
1. **Dark Mode Vibrancy**: Capture screenshots in a room with low light if recording your screen, or use a high-DPI screenshot tool to preserve the glow of the neon blue chips.
2. **Glassmorphism Depth**: Ensure there is some background movement (e.g., the background mesh gradient) to make the glass panels "pop".
3. **Consistency**: Use a standard resolution (e.g., 1920x1080) for all captures to keep slide transitions smooth.
